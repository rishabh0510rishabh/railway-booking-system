About Our Service

Welcome to our Railway Booking System, a mini-project designed to replicate the core functionalities of a real-world train ticket booking platform. You can search for trains, view seat availability in real-time, book tickets across different seat classes, and check your booking status using a unique PNR number. This project was built using Python, Flask, and SQLAlchemy.
